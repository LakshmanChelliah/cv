Lakshman Chelliah

Founder and Owner of Ozerm

Lakshman Chelliah (born October 13th, 2003), also known as Ozerm Erzo, is a Canadian Content Creator. His content consists of hip/hop music, vlogs, gaming content and comedy videos on the social site Youtube, Twitch and other Music Platforms. Born in Ottawa, Ontario, Lakshman Chelliah started his channel as a means of using up free-time and out of boredom. On December 16, 2014, Chelliah opened a Youtube account with the profile name "coolman20068". His first video was made on January 21st, 2015, about the game Minecraft. Early on, his videos mainly consisted of Minecraft "Skywars" and some other trending games at the time. Starting late 2018, Lakshman Chelliah's Youtube channel was filled with music parodies of songs by Youtube Creator KSI. Chelliah's most viewed video is "Inferno", based on the song "Beerus". He is most notably seen in his video with another individual under the title, "Moflies".

Chelliah also used the live streaming service Twitch in late 2018 to early 2019. During that time he accumulated a couple of hundred followers playing Fortnite, Roblox and multiple VR games. Lakshman Chelliah has 3 songs on all music stores as of July 2019, "About U", "Inefficient", and "DataPlan".
